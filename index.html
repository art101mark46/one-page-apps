Art's One Page Apps

A suite of lightweight, single-page web applications, all vibe coded in Google Gemini for fun.

🎱

Pool Scorer

Active Pool scoreboard that covers standard Pool, Killer and Blitz which is a speed game.
🎯

Darts Scoreboard

A minimalist Darts score tracker that can be set for 301 and 501 games.
🚣

Battleships

Online Battleships game for 2 to 6 players with a hint of treachery.
⏲️

Interval Timer

Simple exercise interval timer, enter the number of sets you want, add in the exercises and the time and away you go.
💣

Minesweeper

Online Minesweeper for 2-4 players, a work in progress.
💉

Mounjaro Calculator

A Mounjaro calculator that helps you work out how many doses you have, includes tapering.
🙋

Quiz Show

Quiz Show buzzer systen for two players on a mobile, 8 players with eternal button controller.
🧗

Climb Tracker

Track climbs/routes and add a cash incentive to help encourage the kids./p>

🤘

BSL Training Tool

British Sign Language (BSL) training tool using a dictionary of words and flash cards showing a word or letter to help learn finger spelling/p>

⛽

ICE and EV Tracker

A simple ICE and EV tracker to see how much you're paying over time for energy for your vehicle, additional page to track how much you've spent keeping it on the road.
⚖️

Basal Metabolic Rate Calulator

Quick and easy BMR calculator with additional Hip to Waist Ratio calculator. Work out how many weeks to hit your target weight
⚖️

Ceefaux Studio

Create your own Ceefax web page, with sub pages, custom Fasttext keys but sadly missing stock music. If you can't remember Ceefax, you are a child.
